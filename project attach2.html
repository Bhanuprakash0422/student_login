Computer Science Engineering :
CSE stands for Computer Science Engineering. It is an academic program that combines computer science and computer engineering, covering both software and hardware aspects of computer systems.

    Overall objective of the B.Tech.(CSE) program is to improve the following attributes in students:

  1. Technical proficiency: A solid foundation in the core principles of their chosen engineering discipline, including relevant software and hardware applications.
  2. Analytical skills: The ability to break down complex problems, analyze data, and identify potential solutions.
  3. Problem-solving skills: Applying theoretical knowledge to practical situations and finding effective solutions to real-world engineering challenges.
  4. Communication skills: Clear and concise communication both verbally and in writing to effectively collaborate with teams and present technical information.
  5. Teamwork: Working effectively within a team to achieve shared goals, including leadership and collaboration skills.
  6. Project management: Planning, executing, and monitoring engineering projects within constraints like budget and timelines.
  7. Adaptability and lifelong learning: The ability to stay updated with emerging technologies and continuously learn new skills to remain relevant in the field.
